WELCOME TO PREETHI SRI VEGETABLES AND FRUITS
Preethi Sri Vegetables and Fruits, located in Panchavathi Apartments, Pragathi Nagar, Hyderabad, Telangana, is your trusted source for fresh, high-quality produce and grocery essentials. Our store offers a wide range of products, including fruits, vegetables, leafy vegetables, pooja items, frozen goods, Ganuga oils, and other grocery items, all at reasonable prices. We prioritize freshness and quality, sourcing most of our vegetables directly from local farmers to ensure that they reach you at their best. Our vegetables are delivered to the store within six hours of being harvested, ensuring that you always get the freshest produce possible.
In addition to our daily vegetable offerings, we also provide fruits at wholesale rates for customers who place preorders, making it easier and more affordable for bulk buyers, families, and businesses to shop with us. We visit farms weekly to carefully select the best fruits and vegetables available, and we purchase fruits twice a week to keep our inventory fresh and diverse. Our store is open every day from 7:00 AM to 9:30 PM, giving our customers flexibility in when they shop. Whether you're picking up essentials for the week or planning a special meal, you'll find everything you need at Preethi Sri Vegetables and Fruits.
At Preethi Sri, we believe in making healthy food accessible to everyone, offering fresh and nutritious options that meet the needs of our diverse customer base. We take pride in delivering not only quality produce but also excellent customer service. Our goal is to become a staple in the local community, providing convenience, quality, and affordability in every transaction. We also provide free home delivery services across Pragathi Nagar, KPHB, Kukatpally, and Miyapur. For deliveries within 1 km, customers need to place an order of at least ₹100; for 2 km, the minimum order is ₹250; for 3 km, it’s ₹350; and for areas beyond 3 km, the minimum order is ₹500. We do not charge any additional delivery fees, ensuring that you get your fresh produce delivered at no extra cost. Come visit us today to experience the freshness and variety that sets us apart from the rest.
